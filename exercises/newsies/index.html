Drunk Yoga Turns Violent

At 7:00pm, May 31st, yoga hosted at Impact Hub turned violent when a drunk student broke a window. The individual was attempting to perform a pose called "downward dog", when he feel over and rolled out the window. While the student was not harmed from the fall, as his cat like reflexes from playing way too much foosball allowed him to land safely, glass did scatter the streets. While no one is pressing the charges, however the police claim this could have been avoided if a bouncer had been placed at the entrance of Impact Hub.
